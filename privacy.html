Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Carcorp Australia ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide to us directly, such as:

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Resume or CV for job applicants
  • Contact details for business inquiries
  • Information about your employment history and experience

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process job applications and recruitment services
  • Communicate with you about placement opportunities
  • Provide customer support and follow-up services
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

5. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with employer clients only when you have expressed interest in opportunities or applied for positions. Your information is shared confidentially and only with relevant parties involved in your placement.

6. Your Rights

Under Australian Privacy Act, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Lodge a complaint about privacy practices
