About Carcorp Australia

Our Story

Carcorp Australia was founded to fill a gap in the automotive industry. We specialise exclusively in automotive recruitment: permanent placements, recruitment services, and temporary staffing across dealerships, service centres, and automotive businesses throughout Victoria.

We are hands-on industry professionals who learned recruiting and training from experience. Because of our background in management, sales, and service, our clients recognise and respect that we understand their business. Whether they're a small independent operation or a large multi-national dealership group.

As the automotive industry is our only business, our consultants have professional industry experience. We speak your language and know your business. We look for the same personal and professional strengths that you would.

Our Values

Integrity

Honest dealings, confidential partnerships, and transparent communication with every client and candidate.

Expertise

Deep automotive knowledge. We understand the industry, the roles, and the people who succeed in them.

Partnership

We work as your partner, not just a third party. Your success is our success.

Commitment

Committed to finding the right fit, managing placements thoroughly, and delivering results that last.
